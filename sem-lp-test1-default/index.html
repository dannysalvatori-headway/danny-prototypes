SEM LP — Test 1 Revised (Default / Generic Variant)

Focused comparison: Control vs Revised Test 1 for the default page_variant (generic “therapist” search, no condition/carrier). This is the largest non-brand segment (~28K visits/mo). The original Test 1 was nearly identical to control for this variant — the revised version addresses the 5 gaps.

Variant
Control (default variant). H1: feature framing. No trust signals above fold. Generic CTA. No indication of what happens after click. Cost banner with unspecified “as low as $0.” Flat provider grid with bio quotes, no availability info.
B
Revised Test 1.Four changes targeting each priority gap:
  • Proof strip with human-scale social proof (“1M+ patients matched”) + insurance + availability signals.
  • Sub-CTA micro-copy reduces perceived commitment.
  • Cost banner upgraded with narrower range (“$0–45/session”).
  • Social proof bar reinforces legitimacy below cost banner.
Proven cost banner stays in its original position. No “How Headway works” — sub-CTA handles “what happens next” in one line; provider cards stay close to hero.
